# Keyword Research: Steps - Workout & Pedometer App

**Date**: 2026-02-01
**Topic**: iOS Step Counter / Pedometer / Fitness Tracker App
**Search Intent**: Transactional (app download), Informational (fitness tracking)

## Executive Summary

The pedometer/step counter app market is highly competitive with ~189-194 apps ranking for core keywords. Top competitors include Pedometer++, StepsApp, and Pacer. Key opportunities exist in differentiating through GPX export, yearly insights/wrapped features, and Apple Watch integration. Long-tail keywords around specific workout types and unique features offer lower competition entry points.
