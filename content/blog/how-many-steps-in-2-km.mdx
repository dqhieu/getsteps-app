# How Many Steps in 2 km?

**2 km is approximately 2,500-2,800 steps** for most adults, depending on height and walking pace. Taller walkers cover more ground per step, so they need fewer steps. Shorter walkers take a bit more.

If you want a quick number: plan for around **2,800 steps** at an average height (5'7") and a moderate pace. Need a personalized total? Use the [Step Distance Calculator](/tools/step-distance-calculator) — enter your height and it does the math.

## How Many Steps Are in 2 Kilometers?

Here's the short answer:

- **Walking (moderate pace):** ~2,600-2,800 steps
- **Brisk walking:** ~2,400-2,700 steps
- **Running:** ~2,000-2,400 steps

The most common figure for the **how many steps in 2 km** question is roughly **2,824 steps** for a 5'7" adult walking at a moderate pace (about 4.8 km/h). Your number can swing 200-400 steps either way based on height and speed.

| Pace | Steps in 2 km | Time |
|------|----------------|------|
| Casual walk (3.0 km/h) | ~2,960 | ~40 min |
| Moderate walk (4.8 km/h) | ~2,824 | ~25 min |
| Brisk walk (6.0 km/h) | ~2,596 | ~20 min |
| Jogging (8.0 km/h) | ~2,300 | ~15 min |
| Running (10.0 km/h) | ~2,208 | ~12 min |

## Steps in 2 km by Height and Stride

Height is the strongest predictor of how many steps you take in 2 kilometers. The taller you are, the longer your stride, and the fewer steps you need. Here's a breakdown for walking and running:

| Height | Stride Length | Steps in 2 km (Walking) | Steps in 2 km (Running) |
|--------|---------------|-------------------------|--------------------------|
| 5'0" (152 cm) | 2.1 ft / 64 cm | 3,124 | 2,440 |
| 5'4" (163 cm) | 2.3 ft / 70 cm | 2,946 | 2,304 |
| 5'7" (170 cm) | 2.45 ft / 75 cm | 2,824 | 2,208 |
| 5'8" (173 cm) | 2.5 ft / 76 cm | 2,786 | 2,180 |
| 6'0" (183 cm) | 2.7 ft / 82 cm | 2,642 | 2,066 |
| 6'4" (193 cm) | 2.9 ft / 88 cm | 2,508 | 1,962 |

A few takeaways:

- A 5'0" walker takes about **600 more steps** to cover 2 km than a 6'4" walker.
- Running cuts your step count by roughly 20-25% compared to walking.
- Most adults will land between **2,500 and 3,100 steps** for a full 2 km walk.

A 2 km walk takes between **20 and 40 minutes** for most adults, depending on pace. Here's the time breakdown:

| Pace | Speed | Time for 2 km |

| Casual walk | 4.0 km/h | ~30 min |

| Body Weight | Moderate Walk (4.8 km/h) | Brisk Walk (6.0 km/h) | Jogging (8.0 km/h) |
|-------------|---------------------------|------------------------|---------------------|
| 130 lb (59 kg) | ~80 cal | ~100 cal | ~140 cal |
| 155 lb (70 kg) | ~95 cal | ~120 cal | ~165 cal |
| 180 lb (82 kg) | ~110 cal | ~140 cal | ~190 cal |
| 205 lb (93 kg) | ~125 cal | ~160 cal | ~220 cal |
| 230 lb (104 kg) | ~140 cal | ~180 cal | ~245 cal |

A few quick notes:

- Heavier walkers burn more calories per km — body weight is the biggest factor.
- Brisk walking burns about **25-30% more calories** than a casual stroll over the same 2 km.
- Hills and uneven terrain can boost burn by another 10-20%.

## Tips to Hit 2 km of Steps Daily

A 2 km walk is around **2,500-2,800 steps** — a great daily baseline that fits into almost any schedule. Here's how to make it stick:

**1. Walk it in one block.** A single 25-minute walk after lunch or dinner gets you the full 2 km in one go. Easier to remember, harder to skip.

**2. Split it into two short walks.** Two 1 km walks (about 12-13 minutes each) are just as effective. Try one in the morning and one in the evening.

**3. Stack it onto errands.** Park farther from the store, get off the bus one stop early, or take the long way to your coffee shop. Five minutes here and there adds up to 2 km fast.

**4. Use a tracker.** Apps that count steps automatically (like the [Steps app](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/steps-workout-pedometer/id6746096378)) make it easy to see when you've hit the 2,800-step mark — no guessing required.

**5. Pair it with a habit.** Walk while you take phone calls, listen to a podcast, or catch up with a friend. Stacking the walk onto something you already enjoy makes it almost effortless.

If 2 km becomes too easy, bump up to [3 km](/blog/how-many-steps-in-3-km) (~4,200 steps) or [5 km](/blog/how-many-steps-in-5-km) (~7,000 steps) for a bigger challenge.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many steps is 2 km exactly?

For an average adult (5'7", moderate pace), 2 km equals about **2,824 steps**. The realistic range is **2,500 to 3,100 steps** depending on your height and walking speed.

### Is walking 2 km a day enough exercise?

Walking 2 km daily is a solid starting point — about 20-25 minutes of light cardio. It's enough to support general health, but if you're aiming for weight loss or fitness gains, most experts recommend working up to **5-10 km per day** (roughly 7,000-14,000 steps).

### How many steps in 2 km running?

Running 2 km takes roughly **2,000 to 2,400 steps** for most adults. Faster runners with longer strides land closer to 2,000 steps, while shorter or slower runners take closer to 2,400.

### How many calories does 2 km walking burn?

A 2 km walk burns roughly **80 to 160 calories** depending on your body weight and pace. A 155 lb person walking briskly burns about 120 calories over 2 km.

### Is 2 km the same as 2,000 steps?

No. 2,000 steps is shorter than 2 km for almost everyone. At an average of ~1,400 steps per km, 2,000 steps covers about **1.4 km** — roughly 70% of a full 2 km walk. See our [how many steps in 1 km](/blog/how-many-steps-in-a-kilometer) guide for the full breakdown.
