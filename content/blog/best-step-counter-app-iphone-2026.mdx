## Common Questions

### Are step counter apps accurate on iPhone?

Yes. Apps that read from Apple Health use the same motion sensors. The data is identical across all HealthKit-connected step counter apps.

### Do I need an Apple Watch for a step counter app?

No. Your iPhone counts steps on its own using its built-in accelerometer. An Apple Watch adds accuracy when your phone isn't on you and provides wrist-based complications.

### What is a good daily step goal?

Most health organizations recommend 7,000–10,000 steps per day. The ideal goal depends on your age, fitness level, and health objectives. See our guide on [recommended steps per day by age](/blog/recommended-steps-per-day-by-age).

### Can I see my steps on my lock screen?

Yes. Step counter apps with widgets let you add step counts to your lock screen in iOS 16 and later.
