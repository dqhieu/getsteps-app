# Fitness Wrapped: How to Get Your Yearly Fitness Recap in 2026

Remember how exciting it is to see your Spotify Wrapped each year? Now imagine that same experience for your fitness journey. A fitness wrapped feature shows you everything you accomplished—total steps, calories burned, personal records, and more—all beautifully visualized and ready to share.

## What is Fitness Wrapped?

Fitness wrapped (also called yearly recap or year in review) compiles your entire year of fitness data into shareable, visually stunning insights. Think of it as your personal highlight reel for health and fitness.

A great fitness wrapped includes:

- **Total steps walked** throughout the year
- **Calories burned** from all your activities
- **Distance traveled** on foot
- **Personal records** you achieved
- **Workout statistics** broken down by type
- **Streaks and consistency** metrics
- **Fun comparisons** and equivalents

## Why Your Fitness Wrapped Matters

### Celebrate Your Achievements

It's easy to forget how much you've accomplished. Walking 3 million steps sounds abstract—but seeing it visualized as "equivalent to walking from New York to Miami" makes it real and shareable.

### Stay Motivated for Next Year

Reviewing your progress creates natural motivation:
- Beat last year's total steps
- Extend your longest streak
- Set new personal records

### Share Your Journey

Fitness wrapped graphics are designed for sharing. Post your stats on social media, compare with friends, or simply save them as a personal milestone.

4. **Most Calories Burned** — Your highest burn day

### Interesting Insights

The best fitness wrapped features go beyond basic stats:

- **Best month** — When you were most active
- **Peak hour** — Your favorite time to exercise
- **Weekday vs. weekend** — Activity pattern analysis
- **Goal achievement rate** — How often you hit your targets

### Fun Equivalents

Numbers become memorable when compared to real-world references:

## Common Questions

### When can I see my fitness wrapped?

With the Steps app, you can view your yearly insights anytime—not just at year end. Check your progress in June to see your first-half stats, or wait until December for the full picture.

### Do I need to track all year to get a fitness wrapped?

You'll get the most complete picture with a full year of data, but partial year insights are still valuable. The Steps app shows whatever data is available from Apple Health.

### Can I see previous years' fitness wrapped?

Yes. The Steps app lets you browse yearly insights from any previous year that has data in Apple Health. Compare your 2024, 2025, and 2026 stats side by side.

### How accurate is the data?

Fitness wrapped pulls directly from Apple Health, which aggregates data from your iPhone and Apple Watch. The accuracy depends on how consistently you carry your devices.

### Can I customize what my fitness wrapped shows?

The Steps app's yearly insights are thoughtfully curated, but you can choose which cards to share. Select the stats that matter most to you.

### Is fitness wrapped free?

The Steps app's core features including yearly insights are free. Some advanced analytics and historical data require Steps Pro.
